Walter White

Hello, my name is Walter Hartwell White Sr., but lately I prefer, "Heisenberg". I am a former chemist turned high school chemistry teacher.
Chemistry has always fascinated me, I like to think of it as the study of change. And change has certainly applied for me lately!
I've always considered myself a bit of a self-starter. As I always say, "there's no such thing as half-measures, but tread lightly"!
Aside from being a genius chemist who was hustled out of billions by his former business partners...sorry for rambling there!
Anyways, I am a devoted husband to my beautiful wife, Skylar, as well as a devoted father to my children, Walter Jr. and Holly.

Unfortunately, on my 50th birthday, I fainted at my thankless side-job working at a car-wash for a deranged Armenian man, Bogdon Wolynetz.
Arriving by ambulance at the nearest hospital, I was diagnosed with Stage 3 lung cancer. It was a tough reality to face, especially for my family.
I felt hopeless, until my DEA brother-in-law, Hank, took me on a ride-along to bust a meth lab and I discovered a solution to my problems!
With the help of one of my former students, Jesse Pinkman, the streets of Albuquerque were about to be flooded with a beautiful shade of "Sky Blue".
Out of fear of being caught, I can't divulge anymore! However, you can always find out more about me from my critically acclaimed television show, "Breaking Bad"!
